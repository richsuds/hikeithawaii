    Hikeit Hawaii provides weather forecasts tailored for hikers in Hawaii. Our site utilizes past weather observations as well as automated forecasts to predict what the weather conditions will be for each trail or area. In addition, Hikeit Hawaii predicts the likelihood of how muddy the trails are and how full waterfalls will be flowing (experimental).
